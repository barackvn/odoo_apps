Appointment for services module/app for Odoo

Objective

The purpose or objective of this module is to book services offered by a company on its e-Commerce platform online.

Create different Services from the backend. A service will be directly related to a user who will be booked for providing that service, created inside the system as a product.

So 3 things are required:

  • A user
  • A partner
  • A service product

We can set the availability of a service provider in the backend.


Public user will be presented with different services available and then a time table will come when he chooses a particular service:

Time-slot booking and availability view

Once the user books the appointment for a particular time shown as available, an email will be sent both to the booker and appointee.

A record line will also be created under the appointee user role.

Payment integration with Paypal, Authorize.net , CCAvenue or integration with SMS sending or GMail are the possibilities but not the default features.

Also, can sell mixture of products and services.
